Offset Printing

Offset Printing is the traditional way of putting ink on paper and has been in use for more then a century. With our state of the art Heidelberg presses and our expert press operators, Brookstone Printing’s color reproduction and quality is unparalleled.

  • Cost Effective for large runs
  • Can print on a wide variety of paper products
  • Endless variety of coatings such as gloss, satin, dull and soft touch can be applied
  • Wide variety of options when it comes to ink choices, colors and metallics

Digital Printing

Digital Printing has become the industry standard when it comes to short run, fast turnaround projects. As technology has progressed so has the digital printing process. We can now provide a quality product with a look that is very close to the offset printing. Brookstone Printing has and always will be a leader in the digital printing market.

  • Digital is cost effective for shorter quantities
  • Has a finished look similar to offset
  • Great for short dead lines

Variable Data

Variable data printing is a digital printing process that allows multiple levels of personalization, making each piece look as if it is expressly printed for a single person. This targeted approach focuses on customer wants and needs increasing the probability of success. You can change images, names, addresses, bar codes, promotions or even unique QR codes that direct different customers to different segments of your website!

  • Makes your printed piece personal for your customer
  • Directs potential customers to your company
  • The variety of options are as endless as your imagination.

Finishing Services

Finishing is one of the last and most important steps in the printing process. The finishing process is where printed sheets of paper become a final product through trimming, collating, folding, stitching and binding. Here at Brookstone Printing, we have a state of the art full service finishing division ready to put all the final touches on your product. Here are some of our most asked for finishing options.

Finishing Options

The printing on the sheet draws your attention to the piece, but the finished product conveys quality and pride of your company. We offer a wide variety of finishing options, many of which may be necessary depending on the type of finished product you are looking for. Here are just a few of our options.

  • Cutting
  • Collating
  • Die-cutting
  • Folding
  • Hole Punching
  • Padding
  • Scoring
  • Inserts

Bindery Options

How you want your project to look to your customer is critical so having bindery options is essential. Saddle stitching or stapling says that this piece is organized and is typically used for a smaller number of pages. Perfect binding, on the other hand, is the process of gluing pages together and can say that this is a professional piece with high quality. Perfect binding is great for larger catalogs, journals or magazine publications. Documents, reports and proposals tend to look the best with spiral binding and have the added benefit of laying completely flat.

  • Saddle Stitching
  • Side Stitching
  • Perfect Binding
  • Coil Binding
  • Spiral Binding
