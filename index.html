Services

Brookstone Printing is a high quality, full-service printing company. With our experienced and dedicated staff, our high tech equipment and commitment to quality, you can be assured that your project will stand out from the rest. Total customer satisfaction is our primary goal. We provide a full line of commercial print products, business forms, marketing programs, and promotional products, along with a variety of services from design to mailings.

Design

We have an exceptional design team that will help you every step of the way to ensure that your ideas are fully realized. Your image is our business and we will design a product that you will be proud to display your name on. Our graphic designers are some of the best in the printing industry.

Printing

Our experienced printing staff and state of the art equipment is here to ensure that your project will be produced to the highest level of satisfaction. We stress quality in all of our printing projects and therefore only use Heidelberg printing press's, the best in the world. Whether you choose to have your product produced with offset, digital or variable data printing, we will ensure that it stands out.

Finishing

We have a full-service bindery division that can handle it all from cutting, folding or die cutting to binding and stitching. Finishing is just as important as the printing process and our expert staff is a cut above the rest. We will ensure that your project will be taken care of from beginning to end.

Mailing

We have a complete in-house mailing division with tabbing, ink jetting, mailing list management and delivery with our truck to the Post Office. Our employees utilize the latest in inventory software to handpick your orders and provide customer satisfaction. Being able to design, print, finish and mail out your project all under one roof helps us to keep the cost down for you as well as ensure a timely turnaround.

Product Samples

Brookstone Printing offers a wide variety of products from brochures and business forms to complete custom designed projects. Take a look at a few of our most asked for products!

Brochures

Concisely get your message out to potential customers with a beautifully designed brochure. They are small enough to fit into envelopes and you can put them to work for your next marketing campaign to help bring more exposure to your company for a reasonable price!

Catalogs

Impress your potential customers with a high-quality catalog! Give your customers a catalog worth browsing by choosing from our wide range of cover sheets and finishes. You can further customize your catalog with paper types, weights, trim sizes, and binding options.

Pocket Folders

Look prepared and professional with branded pocket folders. Whether you use them for business proposals, conference handouts or just product literature they are sure to give a great impression.

Envelopes

We can create an envelope for you that stands out from the competition and beg to be opened by recipients. Whether you want a simple one-color envelope or a full-color work of art including custom imagery, we can handle it all!

Business Forms

Keep your business productivity running smooth and organized with business forms. Whether its invoices, order forms, receipts or checklists, let’s help you stay on top of it all while custom tailoring business forms for your business needs!

Letterhead

Show your customers that they are dealing with a professional organization that pays attention to details with letterhead. Letterhead can help you get great first impressions showing that your business is strong and trustworthy!

Booklets

Deliver important information to your customers in a high quality professional package with printed booklets. From product price books to instruction manuals, we can ensure a well-designed and expertly printed booklet that is sure to turn heads.

Direct Mailers

Even in the age of the internet, direct mail has shown to be a highly successful tool in any marketing campaign. Mailers deliver your message right into the hands of your customers helping to drive more engagement. We can even help do the heavy lifting and speed up the time to delivery with our own mailing options.

Business Cards

Make a lasting impression with an unforgettable business card! High quality business cards show your customer that you are professional and care about the details. Business cards are a great tool for networking and getting your name out.

Packaging

Ensure sales with professionally designed and high quality packaging. With our printing and die cutting options, we can help create packaging to showcase your product. Let’s make sure that your product is the first one a customer picks up!

Flyers

Do more than just advertise on social media and email by putting a flyer right into the hands of your customers! Quick to design and small enough to fit into envelopes, you can get your advertisement out fast and to a large area. We can make your flyers stand out with our paper, printing and folding options.

Maps

Even in the age of gps apps right on your phone, printed maps are still bought and stored in glove boxes just in case. With our large offset printing presses, we can print up to a 40 inch map to really show all the details of the area.

Environment

At Brookstone Printing we care about the environment. We've replaced our warehouse lighting with LED units, saving hundreds of kilowatts of electricity per year. We use all non-toxic, environmentally friendly solvents and soy based inks. All of our paper scraps and aluminum printing plates are recycled totaling up to a few metric tons per year. You can have peace of mind that choosing Brookstone Printing for your high quality printing needs is to choose an environmentally conscious company.

Forests

We purchase our paper from sustainable forests! The paper companies we use, buy their products from paper mills that harvest their trees like a crop. They harvest one section at a time and then plant new trees in their place. The process prevents erosion, forest fires and creates a home for wildlife.
