Services

Brookstone Printing is a high quality, full-service printing company. With our experienced and dedicated staff, our high tech equipment and commitment to quality, you can be assured that your project will stand out from the rest. Total customer satisfaction is our primary goal. We provide a full line of commercial print products, business forms, marketing programs, and promotional products, along with a variety of services from design to mailings.

Design

We have an exceptional design team that will help you every step of the way to ensure that your ideas are fully realized. Your image is our business and we will design a product that you will be proud to display your name on. Our graphic designers are some of the best in the printing industry.

Printing

Our experienced printing staff and state of the art equipment is here to ensure that your project will be produced to the highest level of satisfaction. We stress quality in all of our printing projects and therefore only use Heidelberg printing press's, the best in the world. Whether you choose to have your product produced with offset, digital or variable data printing, we will ensure that it stands out.

Finishing

We have a full-service bindery division that can handle it all from cutting, folding or die cutting to binding and stitching. Finishing is just as important as the printing process and our expert staff is a cut above the rest. We will ensure that your project will be taken care of from beginning to end.

Mailing

We have a complete in-house mailing division with tabbing, ink jetting, mailing list management and delivery with our truck to the Post Office. Our employees utilize the lasted in inventory software to handpick your orders and provide customer satisfaction. Being able to design, print, finish and mail out your project all under one roof helps us to keep the cost down for you as well as ensure a timely turnaround.

Environment

At Brookstone Printing we care about the environment. We've replaced our warehouse lighting with LED units, saving hundreds of kilowatts of electricity per year. We use all non-toxic, environmentally friendly soy based inks and solvents. All of our paper scraps and aluminum printing plates are recycled totaling up to a few metric tons per year. You can have peace of mind that choosing Brookstone Printing for your high quality printing needs is to choose an environmentally conscious company.

Safe Chemicals

We only buy environmentally safe chemicals! The chemicals we use are all water based to help protect the environment. Our inks are all soy oil based and our printing plate maker is also chemical free.

Forests

We purchase our paper from sustainable forests! The paper companies we use, buy their products from paper mills that harvest their trees like a crop. They harvest one section at a time and then plant new trees in their place. The process prevents erosion, forest fires and creates a home for wildlife.
