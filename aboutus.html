About Us

Brookstone Printing is a high quality, full-service printing company. With our experienced and dedicated staff, our high tech equipment and commitment to quality, you can be assured that your project will stand out from the rest. Total customer satisfaction is our primary goal. We provide a full line of commercial print products, business forms, marketing programs, and promotional products, along with a variety of services from design to mailings. In 2006, Todd Moser and Brian Mayr decided to start their own printing company, and Brookstone Printing was born. With over 60 years of printing experience between them, Brookstone can handle any print project. In 2018, Brookstone Printing acquired Badger Press Photographics. Operations were consolidated under the Brookstone Printing name in Kenosha Wisconsin to directly serve both the southern Wisconsin and northern Illinois markets. Brookstone Printing has had a long history of serving this area and we look forward to bringing a new energized level of service to our clients.

Our Mission

Your Image Is Our Business

To give our customers the highest quality finished product with complete customer satisfaction.

Our History

125 Years of Tradition

Brookstone Printing was established in 2006, when best friends Todd Moser and Brian Mayr decided to start their own printing company. Todd grew up in his family print shop and Brian put himself through college working at multiple printing facilities.

Our Promise

Providing Solutions & Delivering Satisfaction.

We are 100% dedicated to providing the highest quality level of customer service and satisfaction at competitive rates while striving on meeting the demands of all our customers. Brookstone Printing has maintained long-standing relationships with our customers by delivering what we promise with courtesy and professionalism.

Timeline

Over the nearly two decades Brookstone Printing has been in business, we have never been complacent, we are always looking to move forward.

  • Feb 2006Brookstone Printing is Established and opened for business.
  • Oct 2006We installed a new Four Color Heidelberg Press further improving our printing quality.
  • Oct 2008Brookstone Printing aquired Vilar Arts Printing company in Wauwatosa, WI.
  • Feb 2010Installed a new Heidelberg Supra Setter Plate Maker helping to streamline the production and ever increasing quality.
  • June 2013Brookstone Printing out grew its Wauwatosa location and relocated to a larger facility in Menomonee Falls, WI.
  • May 2015Established the Digital Department increasing the range of products that Brookstone Printing offers.
  • June 2018Brookstone Printing aquired Badger Press Photographics located in Kenosha, WI
  • July 2018Moved to the newly aquired 50,000 square foot facility in Kenosha, WI
  • March 2019Installed state of the art Heidelberg Prinect Software System to help to streamline the production process and increasing quality.
